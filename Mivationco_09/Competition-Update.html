What’s a bracket?

A bracket is a group of competitors who have very similar historical production. For instance, if you average approximately 40-50 policies per month, you will be placed in a bracket with other agencies that average 40-50 policies per month. Bracketing ensures that YOU have a genuine chance to win the competition; we’ve created a level playing field.

Birds of a feather – in brackets together


What if there is a tie?

In the event of a tie, the tied winners will evenly split the combined rewards they would have earned if they had each finished separately. For example, if two competitors tied for first place:

1st Place = $750
2nd Place = $350
3rd Place = $100

Total Rewards for 1st & 2nd place = $1,100

Tied Player 1 receives $550 (1/2 $1,100)
Tied Player 2 receives $550 (1/2 $1,100)

Please note, this means that the next available prize available to competitors is 3rd place ($100) since the 1st and 2nd prizes are being split by the tied competitors.
